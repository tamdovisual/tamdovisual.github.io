About
Đỗ Minh Tâm (Tam Do Visual) is a Vietnamese photographer, currently based in Hanoi, Vietnam. Being passionate about photography since 2007, Tam offers in his photographs the beauty of his motherland.

Devoted to dedication and creativity, Tam's work is being commissioned by reputed clients including Google, Canon, Honda, Samsung, Hyundai, vivo, Huawei, etc. Tam's photos are also widely published by renowned media outlets such as Nikon Asia, Zing News, CNN, The Guardian, etc.

Honored to be called “the photographer who brings the images of Vietnam to the world” (Vietnam News Agency, VnExpress, Tuổi Trẻ, Dân Trí, etc…), Tam uses photography to tell the world the beauty of Vietnam.
